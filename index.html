About Me

I moved here from Florida 8 years ago. Went to IMI (IntelliTech Medical Institute) for my Massage Therapy Degree, and I've been working at Massage Envy for almost 4 years now. I've just seen so many therapist leave and come back after they failed on there own that it became a frightening idea. Luckily for me I'm stubborn and I'm gonna try doing it this year (2015)
